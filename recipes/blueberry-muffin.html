How to make muffins worth dying for!!

Ingredients required:

  • Oil: Makes muffin more tender.
  • Milk: go-to liquid for baking muffins, but if you are dairy-free, try homemade oat milk, unsweetened almond milk or coconut milk.
  • Egg: Provides structure and stability.
  • Vanilla Extract: adds flavor to the bread and make them taste amazing.
  • Blueberries: Duh.

Steps to blueberry muffin

  1. Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, and a little salt in a large bowl.
  2. Add oil, an egg, milk, and vanilla extract then stir with a fork until blended.
  3. Mix in the blueberries, divide the batter between muffin cups and bake.
  4. Serve cold or warm. Tastes amazing anyways!
